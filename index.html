Where Do We Go?

Magic Kingdom Park is inside the Walt Disney World Resort, a massive entertainment complex spanning 47 square miles. Check out how to get where you're going here.

The Magic Kingdom Park is split into 6 themed lands. Click below to learn more!

Main Street USA

Here you enter a turn of the century mid-western town inspired by Marceline, Missouri. Shopping and dining locations such as the Main Street Emporium, Uptown Jewelers, and Casey's Corner Hotdog stand are found along Main Street. You can also board a Main Street vehicle and take it up to Cinderella's Castle before entering some of the other lands.

Adventureland

Attractions such as the Jungle Cruise, Pirates of the Carribean, and the Enchanted Tiki Room make up this amazing land. This mysterious place is also home to the world famous Dole Whip!

Frontierland

Travel back in time to this quaint mid-western town where adventures such as Splash Mountain, Big Thunder Mountain Railroad, and Tom Sawyers Island await you. There's also a lot of great grub to be found in places like Pecos Bill's Tall-Tale Inn and Cafe!

Liberty Square

In this ode to the founding of our country you can visit the Haunted Mansion and The Hall of Presidents. The Columbia Harbour House restaurant is a great place for hearty faire like New England Clam Chowder and chicken pot pie. Also, keep an eye out for the Muppets who are retelling some great stories about the birth of our nation with a Muppet's twist!

Fantasyland

This is where all of your favorite stories come to life. Beauty and the Beast, The Little Mermaid, and Snow White just to name a few. Dining in the Beasts castle is also an amazing experince that can't be missed!

Tomorrowland

Rockets! So many rockets! Blast off on amazing attractions like Space Mountain or the Astro Orbiter. Visit Buzz Lightyear and help him defeat the evil Emperor Zurg. When all of this excitement makes you hungry, stop by Cosmic Ray's Starlight Cafe and catch a performace form Sonny Eclipse!
